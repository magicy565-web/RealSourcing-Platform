# RealSourcing Platform — Complete Feature Guide
*This document is the AI Coach's authoritative reference for all platform features.*

---

## Platform Overview

RealSourcing is a B2B sourcing platform that connects international dropshippers and e-commerce buyers with verified Chinese manufacturers. The platform covers the full sourcing lifecycle: discovery → inquiry → sampling → meeting → ordering → scaling.

**Core value proposition:** Cut out middlemen, source directly from factories, reduce COGS by 30–60%, and build long-term supplier relationships.

---

## Feature Modules

### 1. Factory Library (`/factories`)
- Browse 500+ verified Chinese manufacturers across 12 product categories
- Each factory has: FTGI score (Factory Trust & Growth Index), certifications, MOQ, lead time, capacity, product photos, video reels
- **Filters:** Category, MOQ range, lead time, certifications (ISO, CE, FDA, etc.), location, FTGI score
- **Search:** Full-text search by product name, factory name, or capability keyword
- **Actions available:** Send inquiry, book video meeting, request sample, follow factory, view AI recommendation score
- **AI Recommendation:** Each factory listing shows an AI match score based on the user's business profile and niche

### 2. Inquiries (`/inquiries`)
- Send product inquiries directly to factory suppliers
- Each inquiry includes: product description, target quantity, budget range, customization requirements, timeline
- Track inquiry status: Pending → Replied → Negotiating → Closed
- View all factory replies and quote details in one place
- **Best practice:** Send inquiries to 3–5 suppliers simultaneously to compare pricing and responsiveness

### 3. Meetings (`/meetings` and `/book-meeting`)
- Book 1-on-1 video calls with factory representatives
- Supported formats: Factory tour, product demo, price negotiation, quality audit
- **AI Agenda Suggestion:** Before each meeting, the AI generates a suggested agenda based on your inquiry history and goals
- Meeting recordings and transcripts are automatically saved
- Post-meeting: Rate the factory and add notes

### 4. Sample Orders (`/sample-orders`)
- Request physical product samples before placing bulk orders
- Track sample shipment status with real-time logistics updates
- Compare samples from multiple factories side by side
- **Recommended flow:** Always order samples before committing to bulk orders, especially for products above $5 MOQ

### 5. Sourcing Demands (`/sourcing-demands`)
- Post a "reverse RFQ" — describe what you need, let factories come to you
- Factories submit proposals within 24–72 hours
- Useful when: You have a specific product spec, you want competitive bidding, or you can't find the right factory through search
- Each demand can receive multiple factory proposals for comparison

### 6. AI Assistant (`/ai-assistant`)
- Full conversational AI for complex sourcing tasks
- Capabilities: Multi-turn product sourcing dialogue, factory matching, quote comparison, supplier vetting advice
- **Scope:** Handles detailed procurement conversations, RFQ generation, supplier comparison analysis
- **Difference from AI Coach:** AI Assistant is for deep, task-specific sourcing work. AI Coach is your ongoing business mentor.

### 7. Profit Analyzer (`/profit-analyzer`)
- Dropshipping profit margin calculator
- Inputs: Product cost, shipping cost, platform fees, ad spend, selling price
- Outputs: Gross margin, net margin, break-even units, ROI
- Supports multiple platforms: Shopify, TikTok Shop, Amazon, Etsy
- **Use case:** Before committing to a supplier's price, run the numbers to ensure profitability

### 8. Webinars (`/webinars`)
- Live and recorded factory tours, product showcases, and sourcing masterclasses
- Categories: Factory tours, product trend reports, platform tutorials, supplier Q&A sessions
- **Recommended for beginners:** Watch at least 2 webinars before sending your first inquiry

### 9. Dashboard (`/dashboard`)
- Central hub showing: AI Coach tasks, recent inquiries, upcoming meetings, sample order status, platform notifications
- **AI Coach Banner:** Personalized task checklist based on your business stage and niche

### 10. Settings (`/settings`)
- **Profile Tab:** Name, email, avatar, contact info
- **Business Profile Tab:** Edit your onboarding answers (goal, stage, budget, platforms, niches, challenge) — this data powers all AI personalization
- **Notifications Tab:** Configure email and in-app notification preferences
- **Integrations Tab:** Connect Shopify store, TikTok Shop, or other platforms

### 11. Sourcing Room (`/sourcing-room`)
- Real-time collaborative workspace for negotiating with suppliers
- Shared document space, price negotiation timeline, contract drafting
- Available after initial inquiry is accepted

### 12. FTGI Scoring (`/ftgi-scoring` and `/ftgi-leaderboard`)
- Factory Trust & Growth Index — RealSourcing's proprietary factory rating system
- Scores 0–100 based on: Response rate, sample quality, delivery accuracy, buyer reviews, certifications, production capacity
- **How to use:** Prioritize factories with FTGI > 75 for first-time orders

## Recommended User Journeys by Stage

### Complete Beginner
1. Watch 1–2 webinars to understand the sourcing process
2. Browse the Factory Library filtered by your niche
3. Send 3 inquiries to different factories for the same product
4. Book a video call with the most responsive factory
5. Order a sample before committing to bulk

### Has an Idea (No Store Yet)
1. Use AI Assistant to validate your product idea and get supplier options
2. Run Profit Analyzer to check margins at different price points
3. Post a Sourcing Demand to get competitive proposals
4. Compare factory proposals and FTGI scores
5. Order samples from top 2–3 candidates

### Has a Store (Needs Better Products/Suppliers)
1. Post a Sourcing Demand with your current product specs
2. Use the Profit Analyzer to benchmark your current supplier's pricing
3. Book factory meetings to negotiate better terms
4. Request samples from 2–3 alternative suppliers
5. Switch suppliers only after sample quality is confirmed

### Already Selling (Scaling Up)
1. Use AI Assistant for bulk RFQ and supplier comparison
2. Negotiate private label terms in Sourcing Room
3. Explore FTGI Leaderboard for top-rated factories in your category
4. Use Profit Analyzer to model margin impact of volume discounts
5. Build long-term relationships with 2–3 core suppliers

## Key Terms Glossary
- **MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity):** The smallest order a factory will accept. Typical range: 50–500 units for most categories.
- **RFQ (Request for Quotation):** A formal request sent to suppliers asking for pricing and terms.
- **Lead Time:** Time from order placement to shipment. Typical: 15–45 days for production + 7–30 days shipping.
- **FTGI:** RealSourcing's Factory Trust & Growth Index. Higher = more reliable.
- **Dropshipping:** Selling products without holding inventory — the factory ships directly to your customer.
- **Private Label:** Putting your brand on a factory's product. Usually requires MOQ 200–500 units.
- **OEM:** Original Equipment Manufacturer — factory makes products to your exact specifications.
- **FOB (Free On Board):** Shipping term where the seller covers costs until the goods are loaded onto the ship.
- **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid):** Seller covers all costs including import duties to the buyer's door.
